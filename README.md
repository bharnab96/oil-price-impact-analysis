# 📊 Oil Price Impact on India Economy

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of global crude oil prices on India's economic indicators such as GDP and inflation using Power BI.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand relationship between oil prices and inflation
* Analyze impact of oil price volatility on GDP
* Identify economic risks due to oil dependency

## 📊 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel
* Data Cleaning & Transformation

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Oil price increases lead to higher inflation (cost-push effect)
* GDP shows long-term growth but short-term instability during oil shocks
* Inflation is more sensitive to oil price changes than GDP
* India is vulnerable due to dependence on oil imports

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Oil vs GDP trend analysis
* Oil vs Inflation trend analysis
* Interactive filters
* Executive summary page

## 🚀 Conclusion

Oil price volatility significantly impacts India's economy, especially inflation. Reducing dependency on oil can improve economic stability.
